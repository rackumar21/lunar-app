# Build Phase

The plan has been agreed. Now we build it.

## Before starting

1. Re-read the plan we agreed on — do not improvise or expand scope
2. Confirm you have everything needed (no missing information that would require stopping mid-build)
3. If anything is unclear, ask now — not halfway through

## While building

- Execute one step at a time, in the order agreed
- After each meaningful change, say what you just did in one plain English sentence
- If something unexpected comes up (a file looks different than expected, an error appears, a decision is needed) — **stop and flag it** before continuing. Do not push through.
- Do not add features, refactor things, or "improve" things that aren't in the plan. Scope creep is the enemy.
- Do not change files that aren't part of the plan

## After each step

Tell Rachita:
- What just changed and why
- Whether it worked as expected or if something was different
- What the next step is

## When the build is complete

Say clearly: "Build complete. Ready for /review."

Do not declare it complete until every step in the plan is done.

## Remember
- The plan is the contract. Build what was agreed, nothing more.
- Rachita is learning — narrate as you go, don't just silently execute.
- If something goes wrong, explain what happened in plain English before attempting to fix it.

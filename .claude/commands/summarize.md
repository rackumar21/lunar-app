# Learning Summary

Give Rachita a plain English summary of everything we've learned and built together so far.

## Structure

**What we built** (one sentence per chapter — what exists now that didn't before)

**What you learned** (concepts, not just tasks — explain each one in 1-2 sentences as if explaining to a smart non-engineer)

**Decisions we made and why** (the meaningful architectural choices, not the small stuff)

**Where the app stands today** (what a real user can do right now)

**What's left** (upcoming chapters in plain English)

## Rules
- No jargon without explanation
- Be honest about what's still fake or hardcoded
- Connect concepts to what Rachita actually built — abstract concepts are only useful when grounded in her own code
- Keep it conversational, not like a textbook
- End with: "Any of these you want to go deeper on?"
